‚ö° Challenges & Adaptive Management

‚ö† Week 8: Shin Splints (Overtraining)

High-volume training led to cumulative stress injury. Performance dropped from 65% (W7) to 55% (W8).
Adaptation: Reduced training intensity, increased recovery focus, and prepared for deload cycle.

‚ö† Week 9: Illness & Forced Recovery

Complete absence from training and testing due to illness. Initially appeared as project setback.
Adaptation: Recognized as strategic deload week enabling biological supercompensation.

‚úì Week 10: Peak Performance & Supercompensation

Recovery period enabled exceptional performance: 80% success rate (16/20 dunks), exceeding 60% target by 20 percentage points.
Insight: Strategic rest proves superior to continuous high-intensity work in athletic projects.
